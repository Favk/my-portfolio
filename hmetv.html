HMETV

Project Overview

The Hospital Medical Equipment Transport Vehicle (HMETV) project is an innovative initiative aimed at developing a specialized vehicle to streamline the transportation of medical equipment within hospital environments. This project was a collaborative effort by Team NUTAV, comprising of Favour Kolade, Diego Vester, Youssef Muhiar, Yash Waghmare, and Akshaj Murhekar, under the guidance of Instructor Christopher D. McMurrough.

The HMETV project aims to innovate within hospital logistics by creating a dedicated transport vehicle for medical equipment. Through rigorous planning, research, and collaboration, Team NUTAV is poised to deliver a functional and efficient solution, ensuring enhanced operational efficiency in healthcare settings.
